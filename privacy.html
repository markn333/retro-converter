Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

Overview

Retro VDP Converter ("the App") is an image conversion tool that transforms images to match retro game console display specifications. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user information. All image processing is performed entirely on your device.

Images

Images you select for conversion are processed locally on your device. They are never uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

Photos Library Access

The App may request access to your photo library solely to allow you to select images for conversion and to save converted images. This access is used only when you explicitly initiate these actions.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. No usage data is collected.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
